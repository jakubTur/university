Who is Klocuch?

Klocuch (formerly known as Klocuch12) is a Polish youtuber. He is best known for making video game let's plays, reviews, sketches and music videos - all using his signature high-pitched voice. His most notable work is a music video for his song kruci gang - a parody of a rap song Gucci Gang by Lil Pump. His full discography can be found here.
